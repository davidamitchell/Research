## Items Created

1. **`Research/backlog/2026-03-12-ai-team-size-strike-team-thesis.md`** — Main thesis
   - The core argument: AI multiplied per-person output → coordination cost of each additional
     person multiplied by the same factor → 5-person strike team is the structural unit of the AI era
   - Covers the scout / strike team archetypes, the meeting-as-symptom argument, and the
     economic framing (per-person output $250K → $2-3M changes coordination math)

2. **`Research/backlog/2026-03-12-team-size-limits-brooks-dunbar-network-theory.md`** — Convergent science
   - Brooks (1975), Dunbar (1992), n(n-1)/2 math, US Army fire team structure, Bezos two-pizza rule
   - Three independent disciplines arriving at the same limit
   - Research question: is the limit cognitive, mathematical, or social — or all three descriptions
     of the same underlying phenomenon?

3. **`Research/backlog/2026-03-12-volume-vs-correctness-ai-era.md`** — Quality inversion
   - AI made volume cheap; correctness is now the scarce resource
   - The HBS/P&G field experiment (776 professionals, 3x more likely to produce top-10% quality)
   - Shared mental model as the verification bottleneck; the agentic tarpit; the AI slop tax
   - How to increase correctness rather than volume

4. **`Research/backlog/2026-03-12-ai-force-multiplier-ambition-expansion.md`** — Strategic response
   - Force multiplier vs cost reducer framing
   - AI-native company evidence (Lovable, Midjourney, ElevenLabs, Anthropic, OpenAI)
   - The "cannot do now" list; axe sharpening; coordination roles vs judgment roles
   - Shopify's AI-first prototype mandate as a replicable model
