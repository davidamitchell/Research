## Research Question Framed

The issue posed an informal contrast: LLMs as "random next token generators" vs. humans "constantly generating models of the world." The backlog item formalises this into a research question about the fundamental difference between the Predictive Processing (PP) framework and LLM next-token generation, with supporting sub-questions covering:

1. What PP/PC says about human cognition and what evidence supports it
2. How LLM next-token generation works mechanistically
3. Whether "generating a world model" is meaningfully different from "predicting the next token"
4. The role of embodiment, action, and the Free Energy Principle (FEP) in PP (with no LLM analogue)
5. Implications for consciousness and understanding
