## Approach outlined in the item

The research item decomposes the question into seven steps:
1. Current snapshot of careers pages across 6 major AI labs
2. Historical comparison via Wayback Machine archives (6, 12, 24 months prior)
3. High-profile individual hire mapping via LinkedIn and X/Twitter
4. Job board triangulation (LinkedIn Jobs, Glassdoor, Indeed, Levels.fyi)
5. Hacker News "Who is Hiring?" thread mining (last 12 months)
6. Cross-reference hiring patterns against public strategic claims
7. Synthesis: 5–7 highest-confidence strategic signals
