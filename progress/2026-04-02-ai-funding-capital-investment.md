## Key Findings

The defining structural pattern of the 2023-2026 AI investment cycle is the cloud-dependency deal: Microsoft, Amazon, and Google have structured investments in OpenAI and Anthropic so that AI model adoption growth automatically generates cloud revenue for the investor. This is not passive financial investment; it is a mechanism that converts AI market growth into cloud market growth.

NVIDIA is the largest indirect beneficiary, with $130+ billion in FY2025 revenue driven almost entirely by AI hardware demand.

Current investment in vertical AI and physical AI replicates the cloud-era pattern where application-layer investment accelerated after infrastructure investment peaked.

## Mini-Retro

**What worked:** Using the cloud-dependency deal structure as the organising frame for the analysis made the investment patterns coherent rather than just a list of large numbers.

**What was hard:** Distinguishing unrealised valuation gains (Amazon and Google stake markups) from realised revenue, and making that distinction clear in the evidence map.

**What to carry forward:** When researching investment topics, always separate unrealised valuation gains from cash returns in the evidence map confidence ratings.
