- `Research/completed/2026-03-18-human-brain-prediction-machines.md` — completed; the item concludes that human predictive processing and Large Language Model (LLM) next-token prediction share a broad predictive pattern but are not the same kind of system. Brains use prediction as an embodied control loop for perception, action, and bodily regulation, while current text-only LLMs use prediction to continue symbol sequences and can at most acquire partial world-model structure without grounded understanding or Seth-style consciousness.
