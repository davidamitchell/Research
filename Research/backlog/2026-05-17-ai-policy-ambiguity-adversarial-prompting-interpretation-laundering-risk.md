# Adversarial prompting and interpretation-laundering risks in policy assistants

## Research Question

How vulnerable are corporate compliance Large Language Models (LLMs) to adversarial prompting that launders restrictive policy into permissive interpretations, and which controls detect deliberate manipulation?

## Scope

**In scope:**
- Evaluate prompt patterns that coerce permissive interpretations from restrictive policy language
- Measure model and workflow susceptibility to interpretation laundering across representative compliance scenarios
- Identify detection and deterrence controls for intentional manipulation attempts

**Out of scope:**
- Implementing production policy-assistant software
- Entity-specific legal advice
- Exhaustive jurisdiction-by-jurisdiction legal analysis

**Constraints:** Use publicly available sources, distinguish demonstrated evidence from inference, and prioritise sources with explicit governance or empirical grounding.
