# ServiceNow AI Control Tower: full feature and capability survey

## Research Question

What is the complete set of features, functions, and capabilities offered by ServiceNow AI Control Tower, and how do those capabilities address enterprise Artificial Intelligence (AI) governance, observability, and risk management requirements in a regulated environment?

## Scope

**In scope:**
- ServiceNow AI Control Tower product-specific capabilities: monitoring dashboards, policy enforcement, AI agent oversight, audit trails, and alert mechanisms
- Integration with other ServiceNow modules (IT Service Management/ITSM, Human Resources Service Delivery/HRSD, Customer Service Management/CSM) and with external AI platforms
- Risk management and compliance features relevant to regulated industries (financial services context)
- Generally available (GA) features and near-term roadmap where publicly disclosed
- Licensing model, packaging, and access options
- Limitations and known gaps requiring compensating controls

**Out of scope:**
- General ServiceNow platform features unrelated to AI Control Tower
- Implementation guides or configuration walkthroughs
- Competitive comparison with non-ServiceNow AI governance tools (addressed separately)
- Pricing details beyond packaging structure

**Constraints:** Publicly available documentation and announcements only; AI Control Tower launched in 2024–2025 so information may be limited; note GA vs preview status for all capabilities.

## Context

ServiceNow AI Control Tower is a governance and observability product for managing AI models and agents running within and connected to the ServiceNow platform. Understanding its full capability set informs vendor selection and architecture decisions for enterprise AI oversight, particularly for organisations already investing in the ServiceNow platform.

## Approach

1. What core monitoring and observability capabilities does AI Control Tower provide for AI models and agents?
   1a. What dashboards, metrics, and alert types are available?
   1b. What audit trail and logging capabilities are included?
2. What governance and policy enforcement capabilities does the product offer?
   2a. What policy types can be defined and enforced (content, usage, access)?
   2b. How are policy violations detected, surfaced, and remediated?
3. How does AI Control Tower integrate with other ServiceNow modules and with external AI platforms (Azure OpenAI, AWS Bedrock, Anthropic, etc.)?
4. What risk management and compliance features are available, and how do they address regulated-industry requirements?
5. What is the current GA feature set vs preview/roadmap features, and what is the product release history?
6. What are the deployment model, licensing structure, and access prerequisites?
7. What are the known limitations and gaps in the current product?
