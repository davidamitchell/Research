# Operating model synthesis for split-authority delivery systems

## Research Question

What operating model improves throughput while reducing delivery risk in a split-authority environment?

## Scope

**In scope:**
- Synthesis of answers from Q1-Q6 into one operating model recommendation.
- Throughput-risk trade-offs for governance, queueing, delegation, and escalation choices.
- Decision-ready design principles that preserve control without queue explosion.

**Out of scope:**
- Running net-new primary investigations before Q1-Q6 complete.
- Company-specific transformation plans or tool-vendor implementation details.

**Constraints:**
- This is a synthesis item; major claims must be traceable to blocked dependency items.
- Separate evidence-backed claims from analogy-driven heuristics.

## Approach
