# How Can Institutions Align Strategic Relevance Pathways with Human Low-Effort Behaviour?

## Research Question

How can organisations design institutional rules and operating frameworks that structurally reduce the transaction costs of information discovery, so strategically relevant knowledge pathways become the path of least effort for workers?

## Scope

**In scope:**
- Integration of transaction-cost, information-processing, and institutional perspectives into one design model.
- Design principles that jointly minimise effort, preserve relevance, and improve knowledge-transfer quality.
- Governance and measurement frameworks for sustaining alignment over time.

**Out of scope:**
- Single-team optimisation without organisation-level governance implications.
- Purely theoretical synthesis without actionable design guidance.

**Constraints:** This is a synthesis item that produces implementable institutional design principles and should distinguish direct evidence from design inference.

## Context

[fact; source: https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1468-0335.1937.tb00002.x; https://doi.org/10.1287/inte.4.3.28; https://doi.org/10.1037/0033-295X.106.4.643] Workers face knowledge-seeking as a cost problem, because search, coordination, and interpretation effort shape which pathway they actually use, while organisations face the same problem as an information-processing design problem.

[inference; source: https://onlinebooks.library.upenn.edu/webbin/book/lookupid?key=olbp77182; https://doi.org/10.1037/0033-295X.106.4.643; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-03-15-adam-smith-org-design-desire-paths-ai.html] If the authoritative route is slower, harder to interpret, socially riskier, or blocked by permissions, people predictably substitute lower-effort pathways such as local memory, direct messages, or unofficial tools.

## Approach

1. Synthesize the behavioural and organisational theories that explain why low-effort knowledge paths dominate.
2. Derive institutional design rules that make relevant knowledge cheaper to find and safer to use than informal substitutes.
3. Propose measurable operating indicators and follow-on implementation backlog candidates.

### §0 Initialise

- Question: How can organisations make strategically relevant knowledge pathways cheaper, faster, and safer than low-effort but lower-relevance substitutes?
- Scope: Organisation-level institutional design, not single-team optimisation or general knowledge-management tooling selection.
- Constraints: Distinguish source-backed findings from design inference; expand acronyms on first use; keep citations backed by a Uniform Resource Locator (URL).
- Output: Knowledge synthesis with design rules, operating indicators, and backlog implications.

### §1 Question Decomposition

- Root question: What institutional design makes the most relevant knowledge path also the cheapest practical path for workers?
  - A. Low-effort behaviour
    - A1. Why do people prefer low-effort information paths?
    - A2. Which effort types matter most, search, access, interpretation, coordination, or social risk?
  - B. Strategic relevance
    - B1. What makes a knowledge path strategically relevant rather than merely easy?
    - B2. When do low-cost paths undermine relevance quality?
  - C. Institutional alignment mechanisms
    - C1. Which designs reduce the need for cross-unit information processing?
    - C2. Which designs increase information-processing capacity when interdependence cannot be removed?
    - C3. Which norms and local-rule mechanisms make knowledge sharing safe enough to be used?
  - D. Operating model
    - D1. Which governance patterns keep friction low without collapsing quality control?
    - D2. Which indicators show that the relevant path is becoming the path of least effort?

### §2 Investigation

- **A1. Why low-effort paths dominate**
  - [fact; source: https://onlinebooks.library.upenn.edu/webbin/book/lookupid?key=olbp77182] Zipf's principle of least effort states that people systematically favour arrangements that minimise total effort rather than arrangements that are optimal by an external standard.
  - [fact; source: https://doi.org/10.1037/0033-295X.106.4.643] Pirolli and Card's Information Foraging Theory explains information seeking as a search for the highest expected information value per unit of effort, and it defines information scent as the cue that useful information is nearby, which means people remain on pathways with strong cues and abandon pathways whose payoff is uncertain or delayed.
  - [inference; source: https://onlinebooks.library.upenn.edu/webbin/book/lookupid?key=olbp77182; https://doi.org/10.1037/0033-295X.106.4.643; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-03-15-adam-smith-org-design-desire-paths-ai.html] In organisations, the low-effort path is usually the route with the clearest cue, lowest waiting time, lowest interpretation burden, and lowest interpersonal friction.

- **A2. Which effort types matter**
  - [fact; source: https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1468-0335.1937.tb00002.x; https://doi.org/10.2307/1882566] Coase and Williamson treat coordination choices as responses to transaction costs, especially search, bargaining, enforcement, uncertainty, and asset specificity.
  - [fact; source: https://doi.org/10.1287/inte.4.3.28] Galbraith argues that uncertainty increases the amount of information organisations must process, and that organisations respond either by reducing the need for information processing or by increasing their capacity to process it.
  - [inference; source: https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1468-0335.1937.tb00002.x; https://doi.org/10.1287/inte.4.3.28; https://doi.org/10.2307/2666999] For knowledge-seeking inside organisations, the relevant cost bundle includes search cost, access cost, interpretation cost, coordination delay, and social risk, because each one can make an otherwise accurate source practically unusable.

- **B1. What makes a path strategically relevant**
  - [fact; source: https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-03-10-nature-of-the-firm-coase-organisations.html; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-04-26-permission-safe-rag-enterprise-information-architecture.html] Organisational value depends on information flows reaching the actor with decision rights, while permission-incoherent knowledge estates make even technically strong retrieval systems unsafe or incomplete.
  - [fact; source: https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-16-governance-structures-build-mode-without-full-accountability-colocation.html] Governance works when prioritisation rights, investment rights, and exception-handling rights are explicit rather than left to informal negotiation between separated functions.
  - [inference; source: https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-03-10-nature-of-the-firm-coase-organisations.html; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-04-26-permission-safe-rag-enterprise-information-architecture.html; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-16-governance-structures-build-mode-without-full-accountability-colocation.html] A strategically relevant knowledge path therefore needs three properties at once, authoritative content, access coherence, and a routing model that reaches the accountable decision-maker without repeated negotiation.

- **B2. When low-cost paths undermine relevance**
  - [fact; source: https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-03-26-measuring-opportunity-cost.html] Organisations systematically over-reward visible local cost savings and under-detect diffuse opportunity loss, which means a cheap local knowledge shortcut can look efficient even while degrading broader outcomes.
  - [fact; source: https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-08-productivity-incentive-metrics-quality-review-agentic-ai.html; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-02-hitl-review-volume-bottleneck-rubber-stamp.html] High-volume workflows can preserve visible throughput while degrading review quality, increasing hidden defects, and turning formal controls into nominal sign-off.
  - [inference; source: https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-03-26-measuring-opportunity-cost.html; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-08-productivity-incentive-metrics-quality-review-agentic-ai.html; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-02-hitl-review-volume-bottleneck-rubber-stamp.html] Low-effort knowledge paths become strategically harmful when they externalise evaluation work, quality risk, or opportunity loss onto another part of the organisation that does not control the pathway.

- **C1. Designs that reduce information-processing demand**
  - [fact; source: https://doi.org/10.1287/inte.4.3.28] Galbraith identifies slack resources and self-contained tasks as two primary ways to reduce information-processing demand under uncertainty.
  - [fact; source: https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-03-12-team-size-limits-brooks-dunbar-network-theory.html] Coordination overhead rises rapidly as the number of relationships in a working group increases, which makes small, bounded, high-trust units easier to coordinate than large groups.
  - [inference; source: https://doi.org/10.1287/inte.4.3.28; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-03-12-team-size-limits-brooks-dunbar-network-theory.html] The lowest-friction relevant pathway is often achieved by shrinking the number of handoffs and owners a worker must traverse, while extra repositories or approval steps usually increase path cost.

- **C2. Designs that increase information-processing capacity**
  - [fact; source: https://doi.org/10.1287/inte.4.3.28] Galbraith identifies vertical information systems and lateral relations, such as liaison roles or integrators, as ways to increase organisational information-processing capacity when interdependence cannot be reduced.
  - [fact; source: https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-04-26-permission-safe-rag-enterprise-information-architecture.html] Retrieval systems only increase capacity when identity, permissions, and source boundaries are coherent enough that the system can compute a reliable allow-set.
  - [inference; source: https://doi.org/10.1287/inte.4.3.28; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-04-26-permission-safe-rag-enterprise-information-architecture.html] The strongest digital pattern is permission-coherent self-service backed by named integrators and exception routes for ambiguous cases.

- **C3. Norms, local participation, and psychological safety**
  - [fact; source: https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/institutions-institutional-change-and-economic-performance/160DC098F27349B315A7B376F03E97E6] North defines institutions as formal and informal constraints that reduce uncertainty in human interaction and therefore reduce transaction costs.
  - [fact; source: https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/governing-the-commons/7AB7AE11BADA84409C34815CC288CD79] Ostrom's work shows that locally shaped rules and user participation can sustain coordination more effectively than externally imposed rules when they fit local conditions.
  - [fact; source: https://doi.org/10.2307/2666999; https://doi.org/10.1111/peps.12183] Psychological safety predicts learning behaviour, voice, and information sharing, and meta-analytic evidence links it positively to information sharing and task performance.
  - [inference; source: https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/institutions-institutional-change-and-economic-performance/160DC098F27349B315A7B376F03E97E6; https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/governing-the-commons/7AB7AE11BADA84409C34815CC288CD79; https://doi.org/10.2307/2666999; https://doi.org/10.1111/peps.12183] Institutions align relevance with low effort when they combine formal routing with informal legitimacy, because workers use pathways they trust and feel safe using and because local legitimacy lowers the real cost of the formal path.

- **D1. Governance patterns for low-friction control**
  - [fact; source: https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-02-hitl-review-volume-bottleneck-rubber-stamp.html] Oversight quality degrades when review queues exceed realistic human attention, and better patterns use risk-tiered review, selective intervention, sampling, and attributable telemetry rather than universal line-by-line approval.
  - [fact; source: https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-16-governance-structures-build-mode-without-full-accountability-colocation.html] Effective integrator structures require explicit exception-handling rights, escalation paths, and operational authority beyond advisory influence.
  - [inference; source: https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-02-hitl-review-volume-bottleneck-rubber-stamp.html; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-16-governance-structures-build-mode-without-full-accountability-colocation.html] The lowest-cost relevant governance model is approval by exception, where routine low-risk knowledge requests resolve through trusted defaults and only ambiguous, high-impact, or cross-boundary cases require synchronous expert attention.

- **D2. Indicators of alignment**
  - [inference; source: https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-08-productivity-incentive-metrics-quality-review-agentic-ai.html; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-02-hitl-review-volume-bottleneck-rubber-stamp.html] Multi-metric operating models are more defensible than single throughput metrics because they track quality, review effectiveness, rework, and hidden risk transfer as well as visible speed.
  - [fact; source: https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-03-26-measuring-opportunity-cost.html] Opportunity-cost blindness is strongest when organisations only measure what becomes an immediate visible line item.
  - [inference; source: https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-08-productivity-incentive-metrics-quality-review-agentic-ai.html; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-02-hitl-review-volume-bottleneck-rubber-stamp.html; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-03-26-measuring-opportunity-cost.html; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-04-26-permission-safe-rag-enterprise-information-architecture.html] A defensible indicator set includes search-to-answer time, query abandonment, reuse of canonical sources, escalation rate, stale-access incident rate, disagreement or override rate, rework caused by wrong-source use, and outcome measures tied to the decisions the pathway is supposed to improve.

### §3 Reasoning

- [fact; source: https://onlinebooks.library.upenn.edu/webbin/book/lookupid?key=olbp77182; https://doi.org/10.1037/0033-295X.106.4.643] The behavioural core is strong: people do not evaluate all possible knowledge pathways neutrally, they use cues and effort heuristics.
- [fact; source: https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1468-0335.1937.tb00002.x; https://doi.org/10.1287/inte.4.3.28] The organisational core is also strong: institutions exist partly to reduce coordination cost and to fit information-processing demand to available capacity.
- [inference; source: https://onlinebooks.library.upenn.edu/webbin/book/lookupid?key=olbp77182; https://doi.org/10.1037/0033-295X.106.4.643; https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1468-0335.1937.tb00002.x; https://doi.org/10.1287/inte.4.3.28] Combining the two implies that relevant knowledge pathways must be institutionally engineered rather than merely documented, because behaviour will otherwise optimise for local effort and not for organisational relevance.
- [inference; source: https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/institutions-institutional-change-and-economic-performance/160DC098F27349B315A7B376F03E97E6; https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/governing-the-commons/7AB7AE11BADA84409C34815CC288CD79; https://doi.org/10.2307/2666999] Formal structure alone is insufficient, because informal legitimacy, local fit, and social safety determine whether workers will actually use the designed pathway.

### §4 Consistency Check

- [inference; source: https://doi.org/10.1287/inte.4.3.28; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-04-26-permission-safe-rag-enterprise-information-architecture.html] Self-service retrieval and governance can coexist when the self-service path is structured by coherent permissions, routing rules, and bounded source scopes rather than by unrestricted access.
- [inference; source: https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-02-hitl-review-volume-bottleneck-rubber-stamp.html; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-16-governance-structures-build-mode-without-full-accountability-colocation.html] Lower friction and stronger control can coexist when review-by-exception reduces routine waiting time while preserving explicit escalation rights for high-risk cases.
- [inference; source: https://doi.org/10.2307/1882566; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-03-26-measuring-opportunity-cost.html] The main unresolved tension is that organisations can lower local pathway cost in ways that increase hidden downstream cost, so pathway design must be judged by system outcomes rather than by visible local efficiency alone.

### §5 Depth and Breadth Expansion

- [fact; source: https://doi.org/10.2307/1882566; https://doi.org/10.1287/inte.4.3.28] Economically, the problem includes governance cost under uncertainty as well as search cost, because organisations must choose whether to resolve relevance through local autonomy, lateral integration, or central systems.
- [fact; source: https://doi.org/10.2307/2666999; https://doi.org/10.1111/peps.12183] Behaviourally, help-seeking and speaking up depend on psychological safety, so a pathway that is technically available but socially risky is still high-friction.
- [fact; source: https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/governing-the-commons/7AB7AE11BADA84409C34815CC288CD79; https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/institutions-institutional-change-and-economic-performance/160DC098F27349B315A7B376F03E97E6] Institutionally, durable pathways require rules that fit local work and have local legitimacy, because externally imposed pathways decay when they ignore local conditions.
- [inference; source: https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-04-26-permission-safe-rag-enterprise-information-architecture.html; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-02-hitl-review-volume-bottleneck-rubber-stamp.html; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-08-productivity-incentive-metrics-quality-review-agentic-ai.html] Technically, modern knowledge systems fail less often from missing data than from poor routing, incoherent permissions, overloaded reviewers, and metrics that reward speed without relevance.

### §6 Synthesis

#### Executive Summary

Institutions align strategic relevance with low-effort behaviour when they make the authoritative knowledge path cheaper than its informal substitutes across search, access, interpretation, and social-risk dimensions. [inference; source: https://onlinebooks.library.upenn.edu/webbin/book/lookupid?key=olbp77182; https://doi.org/10.1037/0033-295X.106.4.643; https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1468-0335.1937.tb00002.x; https://doi.org/10.1287/inte.4.3.28]

A practical alignment pattern is to reduce unnecessary interdependence with clear ownership and self-contained tasks, then raise information-processing capacity with permission-coherent systems, lateral integrators, and review-by-exception for ambiguous cases. [inference; source: https://doi.org/10.1287/inte.4.3.28; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-04-26-permission-safe-rag-enterprise-information-architecture.html; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-05-02-hitl-review-volume-bottleneck-rubber-stamp.html]

Formal rules alone do not achieve this, because workers follow pathways they trust and feel safe using, so informal legitimacy, local rule fit, and psychological safety are part of the operating mechanism rather than cultural extras. [inference; source: https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/institutions-institutional-change-and-economic-performance/160DC098F27349B315A7B376F03E97E6; https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/governing-the-commons/7AB7AE11BADA84409C34815CC288CD79; https://doi.org/10.2307/2666999; https://doi.org/10.1111/peps.12183]

Unofficial routes also win when they appear more current or locally intelligible than official sources, so the authoritative pathway must stay updated and context-fit as well as low-friction. [inference; source: https://doi.org/10.1037/0033-295X.106.4.643; https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/governing-the-commons/7AB7AE11BADA84409C34815CC288CD79; https://davidamitchell.github.io/Research/research/2026-03-15-adam-smith-org-design-desire-paths-ai.html]
